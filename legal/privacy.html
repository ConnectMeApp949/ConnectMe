Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, profile photo, and payment information when you create an account or make a booking. When you use our social features, we also collect content you create and share, including posts, stories, captions, photos, tagged vendors and people, and location tags. We collect information about your connections, such as followers and accounts you follow. We also automatically collect certain information when you use ConnectMe, including your device type, operating system, IP address, and general location data to show you vendors in your area.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: • Provide, maintain, and improve ConnectMe services • Process bookings and payments between clients and vendors • Send you booking confirmations, reminders, and support messages • Personalize your experience and show relevant vendors • Display your posts, stories, and social content to other users • Enable social features such as following, tagging, and sharing profiles • Send you notifications about friend requests, likes, and comments • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances: • With vendors when you submit a booking request (your name, event details, and contact info) • With payment processors (Stripe) to process transactions • With service providers who help us operate the platform • When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records (such as transaction history for tax purposes).

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to: • Access and download your personal data • Correct inaccurate information in your profile • Delete your account and associated data • Opt out of marketing communications • Control location sharing and other privacy settings You can manage these preferences in your Privacy settings or by contacting support.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use ConnectMe. You can manage cookie preferences through your device settings.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, secure payment processing through Stripe, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

ConnectMe is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: ConnectMe Inc. San Antonio, TX privacy@connectmeapp.com
