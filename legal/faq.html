Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using ConnectMe.

For Clients

What is ConnectMe?

ConnectMe is a marketplace for finding and booking event vendors in San Antonio. Whether you are planning a wedding, birthday party, or corporate event, ConnectMe connects you with trusted local professionals.

How do I book a vendor?

Simply search for vendors by category, compare profiles, reviews, and pricing, then book directly through the app. You will receive a confirmation once the vendor accepts your booking.

Is it free to use?

It is completely free to browse vendors and explore the marketplace. When you make a booking, a 5% service fee is applied to cover platform costs and payment processing.

How do I pay?

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. You can pay with any major credit or debit card directly within the app.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. If you cancel 48 or more hours before your event, you will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the event are subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

How do I contact a vendor?

You can message any vendor directly through the in-app messaging feature after viewing their profile. This lets you ask questions, discuss details, and coordinate before booking.

Is my payment information safe?

Absolutely. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment provider. ConnectMe never stores your credit card numbers or sensitive financial data.

For Vendors

How do I become a vendor on ConnectMe?

Download the ConnectMe app, create an account, and complete the vendor setup process. You will be guided through adding your business details, photos, services, and pricing.

What does it cost to list my services?

It is free to create a listing and showcase your services on ConnectMe. We only take a small platform fee per completed booking, so you only pay when you earn.

How do I get paid?

Earnings are deposited directly to your bank account within 2-3 business days after a booking is completed. Payouts are handled securely through Stripe.

Can I set my own prices?

Yes, you have full control over your pricing and packages. Set per-hour or per-event rates, create custom packages, and adjust your prices at any time.

How do I manage my bookings?

All bookings can be managed through the vendor dashboard in the app. You can view upcoming events, accept or decline requests, message clients, and track your earnings.

General

What areas does ConnectMe serve?

ConnectMe currently serves San Antonio and surrounding areas, including Alamo Heights, Stone Oak, Helotes, Boerne, and New Braunfels. We are expanding to more Texas cities soon.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the in-app Get Help section or by emailing support@connectmeapp.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Is ConnectMe available on Android?

Yes, ConnectMe is available on both iOS and Android. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.
